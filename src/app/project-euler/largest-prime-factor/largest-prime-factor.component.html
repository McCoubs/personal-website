While not very difficult to implement or understand, finding the largest prime factor of numbers or any prime factors is important when considering the impact prime factorization and factoring in general has on computation and complexity. Computers aren't very good at factoring, so much of modern Cryptography (ex. RSA) is built on the principle of factoring being expensive for computers. Prime factors are interesting, as prime numbers are the building blocks of numbers, and factoring has interesting unforeseen consequences in computer science.
